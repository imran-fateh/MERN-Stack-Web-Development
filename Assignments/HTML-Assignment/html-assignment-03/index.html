The Importance of Sleep

Getting enough sleep is extremely important for overall health. It allows your body to recover, repair, and stay mentally focused. Without proper sleep, both your physical and mental performance can decline.

Sleep plays a vital role in brain function and emotional well-being. During REM sleep, the brain processes information and strengthens memory.

A doctor once said:

"Sleep is not a luxury. It is a necessity for the human body and mind. Without it, our ability to function properly is severely affected."

According to experts, quality sleep improves productivity and mood in daily life.

Experts recommend at least 6 8 hours of sleep every night for a healthy lifestyle.
