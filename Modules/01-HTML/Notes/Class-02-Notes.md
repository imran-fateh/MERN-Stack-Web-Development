## Heading Paragraph HR Notes

## Topics Covered

- Heading Tags (h1 to h6)
- Paragraph Tag
- Horizontal Rule
- Comments

## Important Notes

1. h1 is the most important heading.
2. h6 is the least important heading.
3. p tag is used for paragraphs.
4. hr tag creates a horizontal line.
5. Comments are ignored by the browser.

## Example Tags

<h1></h1>
<p></p>
<hr>
