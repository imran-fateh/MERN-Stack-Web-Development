## Text Formatting

## Topics Covered

- strong
- em
- small
- del
- sup
- sub
- br
- blockquote

## Important Notes

1. <strong> is used for important text.
2. <em> is used to emphasize text.
3. <small> displays smaller text.
4. <del> shows deleted text.
5. <sup> creates superscript text.
6. <sub> creates subscript text.
7. <br> creates a line break.
8. <blockquote> is used for quotations.

## Examples

H<sub>2</sub>O

x<sup>2</sup>

<strong>Important Text</strong>

<em>Emphasized Text</em>
